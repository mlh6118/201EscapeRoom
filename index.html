Welcome to the Escape Reality website

If you have never played an online escape room before, here are some things you need to know:
1. There are many things in a room that can be clicked on.
2. Clickable items will lead to a puzzle.
3. Clues for puzzles may not be obvious, but are within the room and not hidden.
4. A clue or a puzzle in our games will only be used once.
5. There is a time limit of 15 minutes.
6. The puzzles may be solved in any order, with the exception of the final puzzle (treasure chest).
7. Click the door to enter the room.
8. Click the sign to read more about the coders.
